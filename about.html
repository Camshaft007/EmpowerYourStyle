Hi! I'm Emilia

Step into the world of personalized styling with me! I'm an Auckland-based stylist with a passion for fashion that's deeply rooted in my personal background. Having grown up in Europe, I've always been captivated by the power of style to transform the way we feel and present ourselves to the world.

My five years in Paris, the fashion capital of Europe, were especially transformative. I was completely captivated by the effortless chic of the Parisians. Their innate sense of style and understated glamour inspired me to pursue my dreams of becoming a stylist.

Life is Short - Look Fabulous

Motivated by my experiences in Paris, I embarked on an exciting journey. In 2013, I made the move to Melbourne to study at Australian Style Institute and started my own styling business. It has been a fulfilling and rewarding adventure, and I'm thrilled to share my knowledge and expertise with clients in both Melbourne and Auckland. My purpose is to make styling accessible to individuals like you, who may have never considered using a stylist before.

I love helping both men and women refine their wardrobes and feel confident and comfortable in their own skin. Whether you're in a corporate setting or a more casual environment, I'm here to help you create a wardrobe that reflects your unique personality and style.

My mission: "To help women and men unlock their unique personal style and empower them to put together effortless outfits that make them feel confident, empowered, and truly themselves. Let's embark on a journey of self-expression through fashion and create looks that bring out the best in you every day!"

I understand that during our lives, we go through many different seasons, and our wardrobe should reflect these seasons. If you're a woman preparing to start a family or returning to work, I can assist you in adapting your wardrobe to fit your new phase of life...
